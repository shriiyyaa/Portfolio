MoodMuse

Emotion-First Music Companion

An emotion-aware music system that asks: "How do you feel right now?" Users describe their mood with text and emojis, and a custom emotion engine converts this into an 8-dimensional Mood Vector. Songs are matched using vector-similarity, and an Intent Layer (Stay, Lift, Distract, Surprise) adapts in real-time based on skips.
